Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

By Jeromy Chang

A traditional Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 4 ounces pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated cheese, salt, and black pepper. Set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook diced pancetta over medium heat until crispy. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
  4. Reduce heat to low. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta and garlic. Toss to combine.
  5. Remove skillet from heat. Quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the hot spaghetti, tossing continuously to coat the pasta. If the sauce is too thick, add reserved pasta water gradually until desired consistency is reached.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with chopped parsley and additional grated cheese if desired.
